GENERAL POSTING ETIQUETTE BOT 8/8/2024 6:02 AM
Posts
  • P1. Do not spam paid promotional items, paid servers or advertisements. You can promote other discord communities with the permission of Admin or Mods.
  • P2. Do not send lewd texts, images, or links featuring nudity, sex, hard violence, or graphically disturbing content within the text-channels or VCs unless it is relevant to a THREADED DISCUSSION.
  • P3. You will be asked to change your server name and/or picture if deemed inappropriate or in violation of server rules.
  • P4. Do not make prejudiced or hateful statements against any culture, region, or group of people or call for harm to any group. There is a fine line between HATE SPEECH and constructive criticism which Admin/Mods reserve rights to call out and reprimand users for.
  • P5. Back up your opinions with facts from credible sources. Admins/Mods may take action against you for spreading misinformation.
  • P6. Post content in the appropriate Topics Threads Category. Long topic discussions should be kept in threads. Threads are automatically created in the Topics Thread Category for you to have focused discussions.
  • P7. Long topic discussions should be turned into threads to have a focused space for the subject. Do not monopolize a category (like the https://discord.com/channels/1024444752764092486/1024444752764092490, https://discord.com/channels/1024444752764092486/1271079645319532697, https://discord.com/channels/1024444752764092486/1271080013197873252, https://discord.com/channels/1024444752764092486/1024444752764092491 and https://discord.com/channels/1024444752764092486/1223268816981594193 page) or channel with a lengthy discussion which will bury other users' topics or prevent other discussions from taking place. GENERAL CONDUCT ETIQUETTE BOT 8/8/2024 6:02 AM
Harassment & Disruption
  • HAD1. Harassment, disruption, and bigotry will lead to a warning or permanent ban depending on severity.
  • HAD2. Do not use racist, homophobic, transphobic, sexist or otherwise exclusionary language in both VCs and text channels.
  • HAD3. Bullying/witch-hunting of members may result in a ban. Slandering of users is low; attack the idea but DO NOT attack the person behind it.
  • HAD4. No gossiping/discussing of users or their personal problems. Deepspeak is not a server which welcomes gossip about other users or servers. If you are having issues with other users or spaces, take it up with the relevant parties.
  • HAD5. No instigating, abusive name calling or petty and trivial fighting/arguing among members. The first offense will lead to a warning and a possible timeout. The second offense will lead to a permanent ban.
  • HAD6. Please refrain from openly showing and taking drugs on camera in the VCs. Users who are overly inebriated maybe timed-out for 24 hours if they are disruptive to the flow of the room. Turning your video on to mock or disrupt a conversation may result in a warning or time-out.
